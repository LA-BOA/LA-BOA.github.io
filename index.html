(In) The Abyssity Of The Grounds

*Linda Sharrock, voice

*Paul Gründorfer, electronics

(In) The Abyssity Of The Grounds is an ongoing shifting-ensemble with an x variable of members. Instead of working with a fixed group – perfecting the interaction in a known environment – it works with a more organic process of evolution, slowly establishing a language and connection between the pool of likeminded artists, not bound by borders or locality. Since 2011 it’s constantly changing and evolving around the core group of Margaret Unknown, Mario Rechtern and Linda Sharrock.
for more information visit:
http://www.intheabyssityofthegrounds.net/

Arik Kofranek
I am sitting in a multidimensional room
Arik Kofranek spielt eine Improvisation inspiriert von Alvin Luciers 'I am sitting in a room'. Durch iterative Wiedergabe-Aufahme Prozesse wird Klang schrittweise verwaschen bis der Raum und das Lautsprechersystem selbst zu klingen und zu schwingen beginnnen. Das Konzept des Altmeisters wird in der Komplexität gesteigert und um die zeitliche Dimension erweitert

AC/BOY
Compact distorted sound body, followed by unexpected breaks and responses to square kicks drums. AC/BOY music is based on the underlying recording of different sound materials and re-sample in beats made from fragments of memories or done byanything you can imagine .. bass , voices, warao or sometimes ritualistic.

Ⓐusländer
Ⓐusländer is the project that supports the idea of Anarchism as collective functionality and organized disobedience towards any form of authority. The sound performance will be presented in the manner of Anti- Art that rejects framed and conventional standards. Experimental hardcore underground experience will be shared with the audience within indoor and outdoor space. Voice : Marko Markovic Drums: Mikal Maldoror Keyboards: Bri Power Synth: Nenad Stankov
